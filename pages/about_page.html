What is MeSync?

MeSync helps users connect with themselves by guiding them through a simple, personalized and clarifying journaling experience

MeSync offers the opportunity to capture special moments using daily impressions, daily journal entries, and inter-user prompt sharing. It is designed to be user-friendly and accessible on all devices

To get started with MeSync, simply create an account and start noting your exciting and profound moments as daily impressions. Take a few minutes at night to review what you were thinking about today and to add any extra thoughts to your daily entry. You can use the prompt board for inspiration and review your past entries to see your progress.
